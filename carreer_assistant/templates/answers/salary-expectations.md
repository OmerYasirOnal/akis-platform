# Salary Expectations — TR/EN

## Turkish Roles

### Required Field (form)
> 50.000+ TL gross (negotiable)

### Optional / Interview
> Maaş beklentim pozisyona ve kapsamına göre görüşmeye açıktır. Junior seviye için piyasa koşullarına uygun bir paket düşünüyorum.

### If Pressed for Number
> 50.000 TL gross civarında bir beklentim var, ancak rol kapsamına, yan haklara ve büyüme fırsatlarına göre esnerim.

---

## English / Global / Remote Roles

### Required Field (form)
> Market-aligned for junior level, open to discussion based on scope and location.

### Optional / Interview
> I'm flexible on compensation and focused on finding the right fit. I'm happy to discuss based on the role's scope, location, and growth opportunities.

### If Pressed for Hourly Rate
> I'm looking at $15-25/hr depending on scope and commitment level, but I'm open to discussion.

### If Pressed for Annual (USD)
> For a full-time junior role, I'm targeting market rate — happy to discuss specifics once we align on scope and expectations.

---

## Strategy Notes

1. **Never give a number first** if you can avoid it — ask "What's the budget for this role?" first
2. **If you must give a number**, give a range (not a single figure)
3. **For TR roles:** 50k TL gross is conservative but safe for junior; negotiate up from there
4. **For remote roles:** Research the company's location — $15/hr is reasonable for junior in most markets
5. **Always add "negotiable" or "open to discussion"** — shows flexibility
6. **Don't apologize for your expectations** — state them confidently
7. **If salary is too low:** "I appreciate the offer. Based on market rates and the role's scope, I was expecting something closer to [X]. Is there flexibility?"
