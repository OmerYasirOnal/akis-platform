# Upwork Proposal Template

Hi [CLIENT_NAME],

I read your project description carefully and I'm confident I can deliver [SPECIFIC_DELIVERABLE] within your timeline.

**Relevant experience:**
- [MATCHING_BULLET_1 — tailored to job requirements from CV]
- [MATCHING_BULLET_2 — another relevant point]
- [MATCHING_BULLET_3 — if applicable]

**My approach for this project:**
1. [STEP_1 — understand requirements, ask clarifying questions]
2. [STEP_2 — implementation plan with milestones]
3. [STEP_3 — testing and delivery]

**Tech stack I'll use:** [RELEVANT_TECHNOLOGIES from job description]

I'm available [HOURS] per week and can start immediately. Happy to discuss scope details or do a short paid trial to demonstrate my capabilities.

Best,
Yasir

---

## Usage Notes

- Keep under 300 words
- Lead with specific deliverable, not generic intro
- Include 2-3 relevant bullets from CV (match to job)
- Propose clear steps (shows you read the brief)
- Mention availability and willingness to trial
- End with first name (casual, appropriate for Upwork)
