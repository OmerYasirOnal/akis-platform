# Anotasyonlu Bibliyografi — Çekirdek Okuma

Aşağıdaki liste, AKIS Freelancer Marketplace kararlarını destekleyen **çekirdek okuma** olarak kullanılır.

---

## 1. Gale & Shapley (1962)

**Başlık:** College Admissions and the Stability of Marriage  
**Konu:** Stable matching temeli, blocking pair, algoritma garantisi

**Link:** [Math at Washington](https://sites.math.washington.edu/~billey/classes/562.winter.2018/articles/Gale.Shapley.pdf)

**AKIS bağlantısı:** `matching_mode: stable_matching` seçeneğinin teorik gerekçesi.

---

## 2. Sühr et al. (2019)

**Başlık:** Two-Sided Fairness for Repeated Matchings: A Case Study of a Ride-Hailing Platform  
**Konu:** Repeated matchings, exposure dağılımı, iki taraflı adalet

**Link:** [Asia Biega KDD 2019](https://asiabiega.github.io/papers/two_sided_fairness_for_matchings_kdd2019.pdf)

**AKIS bağlantısı:** `fairness_budget`, `exposure_score`, exploration modülü.

---

## 3. Zhao et al. (2021)

**Başlık:** Embedding-based Recommender System for Job to Candidate Matching  
**Konu:** Job matching at scale, embedding + ANN + rerank

**Link:** [IRS Workshop 2021](https://irsworkshop.github.io/2021/publications/IRS2021_paper_6.pdf)

**AKIS bağlantısı:** retrieval→rerank mimarisi, MVP vs v1 roadmap.

---

## 4. Giabelli et al. (2021)

**Başlık:** Skills2Job: A recommender system that encodes job offer embeddings and skill similarities  
**Konu:** Skill→Job önerisi, dağılımsal semantik, skill graph

**Link:** [ScienceDirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S156849462030987X)

**AKIS bağlantısı:** `skills`, `skill_evidence`, `job_required_skills`, skill-gap önerisi.

---

## 5. Zhang et al.

**Başlık:** Explainable Recommendation: A Survey and New Perspectives  
**Konu:** Açıklama formatları, kullanıcı algısı, tasarım repertuarı

**Link:** [arXiv:1804.11192](https://arxiv.org/abs/1804.11192)

**AKIS bağlantısı:** `explanation_json` şeması, `top_factors`, `missing_skills`, `confidence`.

---

## 6. Sonboli et al. (2021)

**Başlık:** Fairness and Transparency in Recommendation  
**Konu:** Kullanıcı algısı, fairness, şeffaflık

**Link:** [arXiv:2103.08786](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2103.08786) | [ACM](https://dl.acm.org/doi/fullHtml/10.1145/3450613.3456835)

**AKIS bağlantısı:** UI "neden önerildi?" paneli, algılanan vs teknik adalet.

---

## 7. International Labour Organization

**Başlık:** Algorithmic management in the workplace  
**Konu:** Algoritmik yönetim riskleri, denetim, insan-merkezli tasarım

**Link:** [ILO](https://www.ilo.org/algorithmic-management-workplace)

**AKIS bağlantısı:** `audit_log`, itiraz mekanizması, insan denetimi zorunluluğu.

---

## 8. OECD

**Başlık:** Regulating platform work in the digital age  
**Konu:** Platform iş politika çerçevesi, güvence, kariyer gelişimi

**Link:** [OECD](https://www.oecd.org/content/dam/oecd/en/publications/reports/2020/06/regulating-platform-work-in-the-digital-age_9e7b4a7c/181f8a7f-en.pdf)

**AKIS bağlantısı:** İki taraflı pazar, komisyon/abonelik tasarımı, sorumluluk.

---

## 9. OECD / ILO / EU

**Başlık:** Handbook on Measuring Digital Platform Employment and Work  
**Konu:** Platform iş ölçümü, istatistik çerçevesi, metrik tanımları

**Link:** [OECD PDF](https://www.oecd.org/content/dam/oecd/en/publications/reports/2023/03/handbook-on-measuring-digital-platform-employment-and-work_f4c975ea/0ddcac3b-en.pdf)

**AKIS bağlantısı:** `time_to_first_gig`, `match_accept_rate`, `income_uplift_proxy`, `repeat_hire_rate`, `skill_progression`.

---

## Ek: Job Recommender Survey

**Kaynak:** ResearchGate. "A survey of job recommender systems."  
**Link:** [ResearchGate](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/272802616_A_survey_of_job_recommender_systems)

**AKIS bağlantısı:** E-recruiting panorama, iki yönlü eşleştirme (iş→aday, aday→iş), kariyer yolu önerisi.
